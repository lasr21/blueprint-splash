Come to Blueprint to learn the fundamentals of web, iOS, and Android development! Blueprint consists of two days:

  • On Saturday, February 27th, 2015, we'll be holding a learnathon consisting of a day of workshops and talks! MIT students will be on hand to mentor you and teach yuo the fundamentals of web, iOS, and Android development. Lunch will be provided.
  • On Sunday, February 28th, 2015, we'll have a day-long hackathon where you can put your new skills to practice! Form a team and experience the challenge and reward of building a project from scratch! Again, lunch will be provided.
