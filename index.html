February 21 & 22 @ MIT
Welcome to Blueprint – a two-day learnathon and hackathon held on MIT's campus. Join fellow high schoolers and MIT students in learning how to write impactful software – no coding experience required.

Learnathon
Day 1 - Feb. 21st, 2015

About 100 students will be randomly selected to join us in MIT’s Stata Center on Day 1 to learn topics ranging from developing iPhone apps to making websites. If you have never coded before, don’t worry! MIT students will teach and mentor you along the way in our three tracks: iOS, Android, and Web Development.
